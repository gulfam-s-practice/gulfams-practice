Garlic Lemon Spaghetti

Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add the lemon zest and juice to the skillet. Stir to combine. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley and optional Parmesan cheese before serving..
